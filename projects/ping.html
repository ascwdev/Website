Ping

  • Date: Feburary 2022
    Languages/Tools Used: C#, Unity

    View Project View Source

    I built this small project as a testament to how deceptively difficult game development can be.

    Upon first glance, Pong looks simple. There are two paddles, a ball, two goals, and a scoring system. What you don't see on that first glance is the mechanics under the hood that make it all work.

    In designing this project, the goal was to understand how complex even the simplest mechanics are to implement.

    Firstly, adding velocity to the paddles to make them move, and then adding key bindings for those paddles to move reactively to your keystrokes.

    Beyond that, using random integers to give the ball's movement some unpredictability, as well as using variables to store each player's score.
